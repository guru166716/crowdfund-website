How CrowdFund Works

CrowdFund helps people raise money for personal, social, and creative causes with the power of community support.

Start Fundraising in 3 Simple Steps

1. Create Your Campaign

Set up your fundraising campaign in minutes by adding a title, goal amount, description and image.

2. Share With People

Share your campaign with friends, family and social media to reach supporters who want to help.

3. Receive Donations

People donate to your campaign and help you reach your fundraising goal.

Why Use CrowdFund?

🌍 Global Reach

Your campaign can reach supporters worldwide.

🔒 Secure Donations

All transactions are safe and verified.

📈 Track Progress

Monitor your donations and campaign growth easily.

Tips for a Successful Fundraising Campaign

Write a Clear Story

Explain why you are raising funds and how the money will help. A clear story builds trust with supporters.

Set a Realistic Goal

Choose a fundraising goal that reflects your actual needs. Supporters are more likely to donate when the goal is clear.

Share Regular Updates

Keep supporters informed about your progress and milestones to maintain engagement.

Use Images and Videos

Campaigns with photos and videos often receive more donations. Visuals help people understand your cause.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is CrowdFund free to use?

Yes, creating a campaign on CrowdFund is free. Platform fees may apply depending on payment processing.

How long does it take to receive donations?

Donations are processed instantly and can be withdrawn after verification.

Can I update my campaign?

Yes, you can update your campaign story, images and progress at any time.
